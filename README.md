# Gamified Habit Tracker

## Overview
The Gamified Habit Tracker is a project designed to help users build and maintain habits in a fun and engaging way. By turning the habit-building process into a game, users become motivated to stick to their goals.

## Features
1. **Achievements**: Users can unlock achievements for reaching milestones and completing challenges, providing a sense of accomplishment and encouraging consistent progress.
   
2. **Coins**: Users earn virtual coins for completing their daily habits. These coins can be used to buy in-game items or unlock additional features.

3. **Gifts**: Special gifts can be awarded for reaching certain achievements or completing streaks, adding an element of surprise and delight.

4. **Gamification Mechanics**: The app includes various gamification mechanics such as levels, leaderboards, and progress bars to keep users engaged and motivated throughout their habit-tracking journey.

## Conclusion
By integrating these features, the Gamified Habit Tracker transforms the way individuals approach habit formation, making it more enjoyable and effective!
